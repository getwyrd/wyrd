## Context

The forces at play: the problem, the constraints, and what makes this a genuine
decision rather than an obvious one. State the situation neutrally — what is true
regardless of the choice. Reference related decisions by number (e.g. ADR-0015)
in prose, not as links.

## Decision

The decision, in active voice — "We will …". Be specific about what is chosen
*and* what is rejected. Use RFC-2119 keywords (MUST / SHOULD / MAY) only when
this records a normative requirement an implementation must honour.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result. The trade-offs
accepted, the follow-on work created, and what this commits the project to or
forecloses — including the cost of reversing it later.
