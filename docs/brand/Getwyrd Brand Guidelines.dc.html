01
Essence
Lower the barrier to entry for new, digitally sovereign cloud providers — by building the foundational primitives they currently lack, in the open.
Correctness is the moat
Reinvent only the differentiating primitive. Deterministic simulation testing is first-class, never an afterthought.
Sovereign by design
EU residency, no hard US dependency, GDPR-by-design. Apache 2.0 + DCO is the house posture.
Owned, not designed by committee
A concrete operator with budget responsibility. Economics that work without subsidy.
02
Logo & mark
The lozenge & the wordmark
A heraldic lozenge — a seal — set against the Spectral wordmark. The mark reads as a sealed record: the immutable, atomic commit at the heart of every primitive. The inner diamond carries the accent; the wordmark is always lowercase.

The family
One doctrine, one house style. Each primitive is a four-letter name in the wyrd lineage, lowercase in product lockups, set in Spectral with its own accent.
